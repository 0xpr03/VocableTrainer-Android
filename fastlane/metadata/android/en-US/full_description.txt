Vocabulary and word trainer to memorize foreign languages and remember definitions

* training progress based on correct and incorrect answers
* multiple meanings per entry
* dark mode (android 8.1+)
* training mode for entering one meaning
* training mode for entering all meaings
* training mode for writing down on real paper
* import and export to spreadsheet CSV files with full utf-8 support

Internet access is used only for one OPT-IN survey.
